Ruh Cafe: A Review

Ruh cafe is a mystery box.

Don't be tricked by its second-hand armchair and boxes lying outside. Ruh is a cafe and not a run-down woodwork shop. If you peek inside, the orange background adds a pop of color that brightens up your day, along with the smell of coffee echoing the room.

Till now, it might seem like a typical coffee shop, but as you walk upstairs, the walls turn into a mural of magical creatures. At the top, you'll reach the religious altar - a room with minimalist furniture and a tatami mat - that brings you into an outdoor, forestry environment in Japan.

Although sitting on the floor is unusual for us chair sitters, do come by and grab a coffee paired with a meditation break.

  • Crowd: Nearby neighbor coming for their afternoon catch-up
  • Price: $$
