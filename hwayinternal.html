Hway Coffee: A Review

Hway Coffee abused their right to freedom of expression.

It is impossible to turn a blind eye to Hway's political stance. With its international beef, being Taiwanese is inherently a political identity. The decoration in the cafe screams far-left liberal vibes with walls covered by posters advocating Taiwan independence, freeing Hong Kong, and supporting LGBTQ rights. Having a coffee here makes you feel a part of something bigger.

Besides drinking their coffee, I savored their milk tea. Hway replaced the typical black tea blend and used Taiwan's tea. The change gave their milk tea has a distinctive floral smell that was love at first sip.
