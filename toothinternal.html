Toothday: A Review

If you happen to be a dog person and plant lover, Toothday is your top cafe choice in Kaohsiung.

Don't be scared of the "beware of dog" stickers because, like a hero and his sidekick, the dogs are here to save the day. By this, I mean that they entertain you while you sip away at your coffee. What distinguishes this cafe from the rest is its tiny picturesque garden right outside the window. With our eyes stuck to the screen, the green plants work their magic and relax your built-up eyestrainâ€”the perfect spot to physically and mentally unwind.

Toothday has a carefully selected specialty coffee for you to enjoy, but I highly recommend you try out their homemade desserts. Although served in a petite portion, its well-balanced sweet and savory will satisfy all your cravings.

  • Crowd: Introverts who comes to hangout with the doggos
  • Price: $$
