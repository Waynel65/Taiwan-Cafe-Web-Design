About & Contact

Get to know


Leix loves to travel and discover off-the-radar places.

Due to COVID-19, he is attending online classes in Kaoshiung, Taiwan. As he grew up in Thailand, being back in Taiwan allows him to explore the city while getting his daily caffeine fix.

Leix created this website because he realizes a lack of Taiwan travel websites that are written in English. He feared that the language barriers prevent international travelers from visiting Taiwan. Hence, the website aims to promote Taiwan's third-wave coffee stores to a global audience and invites foreign coffee lovers to visit.
